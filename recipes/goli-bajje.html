Goli Bajje

Description

Fluffy, crispy deep-fried fritters — a beloved Mangalorean tea-time snack. Made with flour, curd, chilies, and ginger, they're golden outside and soft inside. Perfect with coconut chutney!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1–2 tbsp rice flour (for crispiness)
  • 1 cup sour curd/yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, finely chopped
  • Curry leaves and coriander, chopped
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, rice flour, salt, baking soda, chilies, ginger, curry leaves, coconut, and curd to a thick batter. Add water if needed for dropping consistency.
  2. Rest batter 15–30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil for deep frying. Drop spoonfuls of batter into hot oil.
  4. Fry on medium heat until golden and crispy outside (flip as needed).
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney.
