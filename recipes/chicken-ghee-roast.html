Chicken Ghee Roast

Description

A fiery, tangy Mangalorean classic — chicken slow-roasted in ghee with a bold red masala of chilies, spices, tamarind, and garlic. Rich, aromatic, and perfect with neer dosa or rice. Warning: it's spicy!

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (thighs or drumsticks)
  • 4–6 tbsp ghee
  • 10–15 Byadagi/Kashmiri dry red chilies
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 8–10 garlic cloves
  • Small ball tamarind
  • Salt, jaggery to taste
  • Curry leaves

Steps

  1. Dry roast chilies and whole spices, then grind to paste with garlic, tamarind, and water.
  2. Marinate chicken with some masala, salt, and curd (optional) for 30 mins.
  3. Heat ghee, add curry leaves, then the masala paste. Fry until oil separates.
  4. Add chicken, cook covered on low heat until tender and masala coats it well.
  5. Add jaggery, adjust salt, and roast until dark red and aromatic. Serve hot.
